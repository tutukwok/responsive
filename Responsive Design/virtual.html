Virtual Assistant comes in many forms, popular examples of virtual assistants include Amazon's Alexa, Apple's Siri, Google Assistant, Microsoft's Cortana, and AI chatbots like ChatGPT. Virtual Assistant is another features of AI that contribute to factors of efficiency and convenience.
Most website and businesses have AI chatbots where customers can chat about their problems with a virtual assistant first before chatting with a representative. The existence of this helps cut down a lot of time when people have to wait for an available representative. It also helps people with disability to get the help they need without going the extra steps.
