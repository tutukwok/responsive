Grammarly is a digital writing assistant that helps users improve their writing by providing real-time feedback on grammar, punctuation, spelling, style, and tone. Grammarly is categorized as AI because they are advertised as an AI writing assistant. This app is a life-changing app for many people who struggle with English. Grammarly offers many extensions where it can be accessible across the web so that users will not have to worry about their grammar when writing emails, captions for social media, or finding another person to proofread their paper. Grammarly became essential for workers, students, and anyone who is not confident in their writing.
